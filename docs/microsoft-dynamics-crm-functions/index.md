## Overview

The [Marketing Cloud Connector for Microsoft Dynamics CRM](https://help.salesforce.com/articleView?id=mc_mdc_marketing_cloud_for_microsoft_dynamics_crm.htm&type=5) enables the following:

* Sends to contacts, leads, accounts, marketing lists, and campaigns
* Sends to Dynamics CRM Custom Entities
* Email tracking and performance for individual sends and campaigns
* Marketing automation with Marketing Cloud emails and workflow templates
* Actions on tracking data from Dynamics CRM through Marketing Cloud Automations
* Smart Capture landing page interactions
* Custom charts and dashboards

Use of the following functions is limited to the MSCRM interactions in Smart Capture forms and other custom landing pages.
